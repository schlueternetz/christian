# Memoreel
A free Android slideshow app ideal for showing your memories on displays - like wall-mounted tablets or Android TVs.

It serves customizable slideshows from local storage. Multiple image formats are supported.

Features:
- Works on horizontally and vertically mounted devices.
- Configure multiple slide shows at the same time.
- Quickly start a slideshow from a home screen widget.
- Navigate back and forth in the slideshow to bring back previous pictures.
- Zoom in on the image to reveal more details.
